## Core Philosophy

**Integration Approach**: Layer-based overlay system that preserves all existing functionality while adding premium consultation experience.



**Trust-First Design**: Every element must build credibility and reduce skepticism - assume the business owner is cautious about unsolicited offers.



**German Market Focus**: Content optimized for small business owners in Munich area using FAB (Features, Advantages, Benefits) and 4U principles (Urgent, Unique, Useful, Ultra-specific).

**Dynamic Personalization**: All content must be intelligently adapted based on analysis of the existing website to create maximum relevance and impact.
